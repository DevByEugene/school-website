Our School Programs

We offer comprehensive Cambridge-certified programs designed to prepare students for academic excellence and future success. Each program is tailored to different age groups and academic levels.

Primary Level (Cambridge)

At Crestfield, we offer a comprehensive Cambridge Primary program tailored specifically for students in Grades 4 to 6. This program lays a strong foundation in core subjects such as English, Mathematics, and Science. We emphasize not only academic excellence but also foster curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking to nurture lifelong learners.

Key Features:

  • Foundation in English, Mathematics, and Science
  • Development of critical thinking skills
  • Interactive and engaging learning methods
  • Regular assessments and progress tracking

Lower Secondary (Cambridge)

Our Cambridge Lower Secondary curriculum is designed for students in Grades 7 to 9 and offers a broad and balanced education. The program promotes inquiry-based learning, enabling students to develop skills in problem-solving, analysis, and communication, preparing them for higher secondary education with confidence.

Key Features:

  • Broad and balanced curriculum
  • Inquiry-based learning approach
  • Development of analytical and communication skills
  • Preparation for IGCSE level
Get More Information

IGCSE (Cambridge)

The two-year Cambridge IGCSE program is delivered online for students aged 16 to 18. It provides a rigorous and internationally recognized curriculum that develops knowledge, skills, and understanding through coursework and final examinations. Our expert tutors guide students through the coursework with personalized support to ensure academic success.

Key Features:

  • Internationally recognized qualification
  • Online delivery with expert tutors
  • Rigorous curriculum with coursework and exams
  • Personalized support and guidance
  • Wide range of subject options
Get More Information

A-Levels (Cambridge & Edexcel)

We offer a world-class A-Level program recognized by top universities globally. Delivered over two years, the program is designed for senior students aiming for university admission worldwide. Students have access to Cambridge and Edexcel certifications, with a focus on depth of knowledge, research skills, and academic excellence.

Key Features:

  • Globally recognized by top universities
  • Cambridge and Edexcel certification options
  • In-depth subject knowledge development
  • Research skills and academic excellence focus
  • University admission preparation
